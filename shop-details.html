Product Description

Okranchy Original — Salt and Vinegar is our flagship flavor — the one that started it all. Each chip begins with fresh okra hand-selected from local farms around Valenzuela City and nearby provinces. The okra is carefully sliced, lightly seasoned with natural sea salt and tangy vinegar, then cooked to achieve that signature satisfying crunch with a zesty twist that sets Okranchy apart from ordinary vegetable chips.

What makes Okranchy different? We believe healthier snacking shouldn't mean sacrificing taste. Our okra chips contain zero artificial preservatives, zero MSG, and zero trans fat. They're naturally rich in dietary fiber, vitamins A and C, and antioxidants — making them a smart choice for students, professionals, health-conscious families, and anyone who loves a good crunch.

Available in Small (50g), Medium (100g), and Large (150g) packs. Developed by Ani Co., a student entrepreneurship group from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela (PLV) — every bag represents quality, innovation, and Filipino pride.
