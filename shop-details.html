Product Description

Okranchy Original is our flagship flavor — the one that started it all. Each chip begins with fresh okra hand-selected from local farms around Valenzuela City and nearby provinces. The okra is carefully sliced, lightly seasoned with natural sea salt, and then cooked to achieve that signature satisfying crunch that sets Okranchy apart from ordinary vegetable chips.

What makes Okranchy different? We believe that healthier snacking shouldn't mean sacrificing taste. Our okra chips contain zero artificial preservatives, zero MSG, and zero trans fat. They're naturally rich in dietary fiber, vitamins A and C, and antioxidants — making them a smart choice for students, professionals, health-conscious families, and anyone who loves a good crunch. Available in Small (50g), Medium (100g), and Large (200g) packs, Okranchy Original is perfect for on-the-go snacking, baon, merienda, or sharing with your barkada. Developed by Nextgsight, a student entrepreneurship group from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela (PLV), every bag of Okranchy represents quality, innovation, and Filipino pride.
