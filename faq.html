FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Okranchy different from other vegetable chips?

Okranchy is made from 100% fresh, locally-sourced okra — not powdered or processed vegetables. Our unique preparation method preserves the natural crunch and nutrients of okra while creating a satisfying, guilt-free snack. Unlike typical chips, Okranchy contains zero artificial preservatives, zero MSG, and zero trans fat. Plus, it's crafted by Nextgsight, a dedicated student group from PLV, ensuring quality and innovation in every bag.

Where can I buy Okranchy products?

You can purchase Okranchy directly from our website for delivery, or visit us at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela (PLV) campus during our selling hours. We also participate in local bazaars, weekend markets, and community events around Valenzuela City. Follow our social media pages to stay updated on where we'll be next!

How long do Okranchy chips stay fresh?

Our okra chips have a shelf life of 3 months when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, we recommend consuming within 1 week for the best crunch and flavor. The resealable packaging helps maintain freshness — just make sure to seal it tightly after each snack session!

Do you offer bulk orders or wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer special bulk pricing for events, corporate giveaways, resellers, and partnership opportunities. Whether you need 50 bags for a birthday party or 500+ for a business, we're happy to accommodate. Contact us through the form on our Contact page or email nextgsight.okranchy@plv.edu.ph for a custom quote.

Are Okranchy chips suitable for people with dietary restrictions?

Okranchy Original is naturally vegan and gluten-free. However, some flavored variants (like Cheese) may contain dairy. We're working on expanding our range to include more dietary-friendly options. If you have specific allergies or concerns, please check the ingredients listed on each product page or contact us directly — we're transparent about what goes into every bag.
