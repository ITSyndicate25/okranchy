Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Okranchy different from other vegetable chips?

Okranchy is made from 100% fresh, locally-sourced okra — not powdered or processed vegetables. Our unique preparation method preserves the natural crunch and nutrients of okra while creating a satisfying, guilt-free snack. Unlike typical chips, Okranchy contains zero artificial preservatives, zero MSG, and zero trans fat.

Where can I buy Okranchy products?

You can purchase Okranchy directly from our website for delivery, or visit us at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela (PLV) campus during our selling hours. We also participate in local bazaars, weekend markets, and community events around Valenzuela City. Follow our social media pages to stay updated!

How long do Okranchy chips stay fresh?

Our okra chips have a shelf life of 3 months when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, we recommend consuming within 1 week for the best crunch and flavor. The resealable packaging helps maintain freshness.

Do you offer bulk orders or wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer special bulk pricing for events, corporate giveaways, resellers, and partnership opportunities. Whether you need 50 bags for a birthday party or 500+ for a business, we're happy to accommodate. Contact us through our Contact page for a custom quote.

Are Okranchy chips suitable for dietary restrictions?

Okranchy Original is naturally vegan and gluten-free. However, some flavored variants (like Cheese) may contain dairy. If you have specific allergies or concerns, please check the ingredients listed on each product page or contact us directly.
