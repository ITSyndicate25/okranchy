About Okranchy

Turning Fresh Okra Into Your Favorite Crunchy Snack

Okranchy was born from a simple idea: what if we could transform the humble okra into a delicious, crunchy, and nutritious snack? Developed by Ani Co., a group of entrepreneurial students from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela (PLV), Okranchy is our answer to the growing demand for healthier Filipino snack alternatives.

Premium Quality Ingredients

Made from fresh, locally-sourced okra with no artificial preservatives or MSG

Crafted with Care

Every batch is carefully prepared to ensure the perfect crunch and flavor in every chip
